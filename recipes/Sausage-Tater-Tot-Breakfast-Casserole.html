Sausage Tater Tot Breakfast Casserole

Description

This sausage tater tot breakfast casserole is great for brunch and can be made ahead the
night before.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork breakfast sausage
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 pounds tater tots

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

    2. Step 2

  2. Place sausage in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly
    brown, about 5 to 7 minutes. Drain, and spread evenly in the bottom of a 9x13-
    inch pan. Sprinkle cheese over sausage.

    3. Step 3

  3. Beat milk and eggs together in a large bowl. Pour over cheese. Casserole can be
    refrigerated overnight at this point if desired. Top with tater tots.

    4. Step 4

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes. Cool briefly before serving.
